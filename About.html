About Taesflicks

Taesflicks was born out of a passion for photography that began in high school. It all started in my photo 1 class, where I discovered a love for capturing moments and telling stories through images. This passion led me to pursue AP photography, where I honed my skills and explored various techniques.

Before college, I invested in my first camera and began to document my experiences on campus. Photography became more than just a hobby; it became a way for me to express myself and share my perspective with the world.

Driven by this passion and the desire to turn my hobby into a profession, Taesflicks was founded. Our mission is to provide stunning photography that captures the beauty of life and evokes emotion in viewers. We aim to tell stories, preserve memories, and inspire others through our work.
